Before connecting Netlify MCP, enable the Model Context Protocol feature in your Slack app settings. This allows your Slack app to share context with LLMs and agents.

<Steps>
1. ### Enable MCP in your Slack app

   - Go to [api.slack.com/apps](https://api.slack.com/apps) and open your app.
   - In the left sidebar, under **Features**, click **Agents & AI Apps**.
   - Under **Model Context Protocol**, toggle it on.

   {/* TODO: add screenshot — alt: "Enable Model Context Protocol in Slack API dashboard", original src: @/assets/docs/agent-connectors/netlifymcp/enable-mcp.png */}
2. ### Connect in Scalekit

   In [Scalekit dashboard](https://app.scalekit.com), go to **AgentKit** > **Connections** > **Create Connection**. Find **Netlify MCP** and click **Create**.
</Steps>
