By default, Scalekit sends verification emails through its own email provider. You can also use your own email provider instead. This lets you send emails from your own email address and use your own branding.

<Card title="Contact Support" icon="slack">
    This feature is currently available upon request. [Contact our support team](/support/contact-us) to have this feature enabled for your account.
</Card>
