## What you can do

Connect this agent connector to let your agent:

- **Send natural-language queries** — ask about audiences, journeys, campaigns, and Analytics data in plain English; the AI assistant translates your query into Adobe API calls
- **Manage Adobe context** — switch organizations, Experience Platform sandboxes, and Customer Journey Analytics dataviews for your session
- **Track async tasks** — submit long-running operations and poll for results using task IDs and cursors
- **Configure session preferences** — persist your sandbox, dataview, and organization settings across sessions for up to 90 days
- **Submit and rate feedback** — send thumbs up/down ratings, flag harmful content, and submit free-text comments about AI responses
