## What you can do

Connect this agent connector to let your agent:

- **Search meetings** — Search meetings by keyword, date range, or tag
- **Search transcripts** — Search across meeting transcripts for specific phrases or topics
- **Read meeting details** — Fetch full meeting details including notes, attendees, and AI insights for up to 50 meetings at once
- **Get full transcripts** — Retrieve word-by-word transcripts with speaker attribution and timestamps
- **Search action items** — Find open or completed action items, filter by assignee, date range, or status
- **Search calendar events** — Search calendar events connected to Circleback meetings
- **Search emails** — Search emails associated with contacts and meetings
- **Find profiles and domains** — Look up contact profiles by name or company domains by search term
- **List tags** — Retrieve all tags in the workspace for filtering meetings and action items
