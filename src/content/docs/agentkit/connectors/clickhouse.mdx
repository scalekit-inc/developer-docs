## What you can do

Connect this agent connector to let your agent:

- **Get organization cost, organization details, service details** — Get billing and usage cost data for an organization over a date range (max 31 days)
- **List databases, tables, service backups** — List all databases in a ClickHouse service
- **Run select query** — Execute a read-only SELECT query against a ClickHouse service
