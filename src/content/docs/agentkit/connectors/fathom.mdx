## What you can do

Connect this agent connector to let your agent:

- **List meetings and recording types** — Retrieve past meetings, meeting types, teams, and team members from Fathom
- **Get recording transcripts and summaries** — Fetch the full transcript or AI-generated summary for a specific Fathom recording
- **Create webhook subscriptions** — Register a webhook URL to receive meeting data (summaries, transcripts, action items) after each recording
- **Delete webhooks** — Remove an existing webhook subscription by its ID
