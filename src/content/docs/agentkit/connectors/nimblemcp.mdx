## What you can do

Connect this agent connector to let your agent:

- **Search the web** — Search across multiple search engines with configurable result richness and geo-targeting
- **Extract web content** — Parse and extract structured content from any URL synchronously
- **Async extraction** — Start background URL extraction tasks and retrieve results when ready
- **Crawl and map websites** — Discover all pages on a site by crawling its pages and sitemap
- **Run extraction agents** — Generate, run, and manage custom extraction agents for complex scraping tasks
- **Manage crawl jobs** — Start, monitor, and cancel website crawl jobs
