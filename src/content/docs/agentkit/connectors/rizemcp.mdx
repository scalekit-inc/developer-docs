## What you can do

Connect this agent connector to let your agent:

- **Track time** — Create, update, approve, or reject time entries; generate AI-suggested entries and regenerate them
- **Manage projects and clients** — Full CRUD for clients, projects, and tasks
- **Organize with labels** — Create, update, and delete labels to categorize work
- **View analytics** — Query personal and team time allocation, apps used, events, and productivity signals
- **Manage team** — List members, update roles, and invite new team members
- **Report profitability** — Access org-level and contract-level profitability trends and create expense or revenue entries
- **Run reports** — List and retrieve report runs for scheduled reporting
