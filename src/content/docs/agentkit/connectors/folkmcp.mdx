## What you can do

Connect this agent connector to let your agent:

- **Update folk** — Update an existing person (contact) record in Folk CRM with new native or custom field values
- **Search folk** — Search for people (contacts) in the Folk CRM workspace by name, email, or custom field values
- **Get folk** — Retrieves the complete structure of the folk workspace: groups, entity types per group, native fields, custom field definitions, pipeline views, and workspace members
- **Create folk** — Create a new person (contact) record in the Folk CRM workspace with native and custom field values
